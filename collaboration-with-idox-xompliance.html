European Expansion

CRC & Idox Compliance Strategic Collaboration

Bringing comprehensive compliance solutions across the UK and Europe

CRC LAUNCHES COLLABORATION WITH IDOX COMPLIANCE

AND EXPANDS ITS BUSINESS TO EUROPE

April 14, 2016 London, UK

London, UK, April 14, 2016 London specialist consultancy firm, Compliance and Risk Consultancy (CRC) - The Compliance Experts, today announced an agreement for strategic collaboration with Berlin and Brussels-based company- Idox Compliance.

Leading European Provider

Idox Compliance is a leading provider of compliance solutions in Europe, providing compliance training expertise to corporate and public sector customers across all sectors and sizes.

Exclusive UK Partnership

CRC is the only UK Company involved in this type of collaboration with Idox, bringing unique opportunities for British businesses.

Collaboration Benefits

The collaboration will enable both partners to add complementary services to their existing offering, assisting clients to better meet their increasing compliance needs and regulatory requirements.

Advice & Consultancy
E-Learning Software
On-Going Training
Supporting SMEs Across Europe

In addition to serving their existing clients, as part of the collaboration, CRC and Idox Compliance will work together to provide small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in the UK and across Europe with a broad range of compliance related services, including advice and consultancy, e-learning software and on-going training.

The new proposition is particularly suitable for UK companies in need of continental European expertise, especially if they wish to expand their businesses into the European Union.

"We are delighted to have secured a collaboration with Idox Compliance. Both CRC and Idox will be able to provide enhanced benefits and services to our clients through dedicated professional services. The combination will create a distinctive customer experience."

Yousouf Jhugroo

CEO, Compliance and Risk Consultancy (CRC)

"Compliance is an ever increasing topic for organisations of all sizes and sectors. Together with CRC we firmly believe that we will provide these organisations with a compelling offer, enabling them to conduct business in line with regulatory requirements."

Michael Kayser

Managing Director, Idox Compliance

About CRC

A specialised, independent consultancy practice, CRC prides itself on offering clients a bespoke service to suit unique needs. The firm brings together highly qualified and experienced compliance experts across a wide range of sectors.

CRC's expert team is spread across the UK from the north of Scotland, South West of England and Northern Ireland, with a Head Office in London.

www.compliance-experts.co.uk
About Idox Compliance

Idox Compliance is a leading provider of compliance communication, training services and consultancy. Idox tailors compliance solutions to meet customer's specific requirements, ensuring that employees understand the risk and compliance issues affecting the business.

Idox Compliance is a member of the Idox group of companies, a supplier of specialist document management collaboration solutions to the public sector and highly regulated industries worldwide.

www.compliance.idoxgroup.com
CRC Methodology

CRC provides clients with a structured methodology for thinking about compliance and risk, building customer satisfaction and improved performance into the compliance process. We work with our clients to help them develop and implement compliance strategies tailored to their specific business models and needs. In addition, CRC provides the necessary tools and legal competence to assess and monitor compliance and manage risk.
