Welcome to Care Quality Connect

Experience at the Highest Level of Senior Living

Care Quality Connect was formed in 2015 with a vision to provide dignified, healthy, and secure lives to seniors of our society, through person-centered quality care enriching their lives by nurturing spiritual and human values with a focus on community-based care.

Our Services

We Provide Great Care for the Elderly

Premium

Independent Living

CQC Independent Living program is meant for seniors who are active and agile but prefer to stay away from the errands, tasks, responsibilities, tensions, and hectic schedules of life. The program is best suited for those looking for a peaceful and joyful retired life. It is thoughtfully designed to cater to the requirements of active elders who prefer independent living and do not have major health issues.

For seniors who need assistance with daily activities, we provide fine home care and best-in-class accommodation in fully-furnished residential units with senior-friendly design, state-of-the-art facilities, and 24×7 nursing support.

24/7 Care

Long Term Care

Long term care services are designed to provide comprehensive care for elders who suffer from chronic ailments and disabilities and require full-time assistance over an extended period. Each resident receives a personalized care plan based on their current health condition.

Specialized nursing care is provided, with proper management of chronic illnesses in consultation with specialty hospitals. Doctors visit twice weekly, routine medical evaluations are held monthly, and the nurse's station functions 24×7 to ensure complete support.

Flexible

Short Term Care

CQC's short-term skilled care & rehab program is designed to meet the diverse medical needs of residents efficiently. It combines skilled nursing care in a home-like, comfortable atmosphere where residents feel at ease.

With state-of-the-art facilities and highly qualified nursing personnel, we provide top-quality after-care services that ensure recovery, comfort, and peace of mind.

Respite

Respite Care

Our respite care offers a much-needed break for caregivers, giving them time to rest while ensuring their loved ones are in safe, compassionate hands. The duration of stay is entirely flexible and tailored to each client's needs.

Residents enjoy a change from their usual routine with full support from trained staff and paramedics. Our advanced facilities, attentive care, and personalized services ensure their stay is comfortable, enjoyable, and enriching.
