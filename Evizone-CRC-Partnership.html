Evizone Announces Key Partnership with

Compliance & Risk Consultancy

May 28, 2018 Montreal, Quebec

MONTREAL, QUEBEC, May 28, 2018 Evizone Ltd., the software and services provider offering enterprise solutions in communications governance, secure messaging and regulatory compliance, is proud to announce that it has signed a partnership with London-based, full-service compliance and risk management consultancy, Compliance & Risk Consultancy (CRC).

75+ Years Experience

CRC's team brings over 75 years of combined experience in designing, implementing and managing comprehensive compliance strategies.

Military-Grade Security

Evizone Communications Governance (ECG) provides military-grade advanced compliance supervision and e-discovery services.

Multi-Industry Expertise

CRC will now offer their clients the use of Evizone Communications Governance (ECG), serving a wide range of verticals including:

Public Transportation
Finance
Healthcare
Broadcasting
Renewable Energy

"We are excited to partner with Evizone to offer our clients an exceptional product. ECG offers functionality that other more widely used compliance solutions lack. We will work closely with Evizone to ensure smooth implementation of the product at the customer sites."

Yousouf Jhugroo, Founder and CEO, CRC

"We are proud that one of the leading London-based compliance consultancies, Compliance and Risk Consultancy, has decided to partner with Evizone. Their team of experts in governance, law, compliance and risk will be strengthened by the strongest and securest communications governance system commercially available."

Bill Wells, Chairman, Evizone

ECG Key Features

Automated review capabilities
Pre-review of outgoing communications
Policy-defined sampling criteria
Document retention management
System-wide legal hold
User-managed discovery

ECG is designed for self-service and allows for automated review, pre-review of outgoing communications, policy-defined sampling criteria, document retention management, system-wide legal hold and user-managed discovery deliver power, simplicity and comprehensive coverage.

"Evizone efficiently and effectively addresses many of the growing compliance requirements of its regulated, unregistered and unregulated customers. Evizone is also a great added value for CRC to ensure all their client's needs are met," added Mr. Jhugroo.

About Evizone Ltd.

Evizone Ltd. is a revolutionary secure communications software and service provider based in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Evizone offers innovative enterprise solutions in secure messaging, regulatory compliance and communications governance. Evizone's services protect organizations through best in class security, recipient controls, life cycle management, discovery management, compliance management, archiving, and complete audit records against the enormous damage caused by communications breaches. Evizone's patented technologies offer a level of security impossible to obtain with conventional or encrypted email.
