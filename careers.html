Legal Consultant - Broadcasting & Regulatory Services

London, UK / Asia Full-Time Closed

Position Closed: 31st January 2017

With a growing presence across Asia, CRC is in search of a talented professional for its expanding Asian clients in the IP and Broadcasting sector.

About the Role

You will be working primarily with some major broadcasting players in the Asian markets and European ones. You will have responsibility for all aspects of broadcasting, legal and compliance affairs in CRC, including:

  • Government relations and corporate responsibility
  • Public relations and employee relations
  • Commercial, corporate, regulatory and compliance matters
  • Drafting and negotiating commercial contracts and supply agreements
  • IP issues liaison with external Counsel
  • Advising clients on broadcasting needs and compliance manual development

Ideal Candidate Profile

Qualifications & Experience:

  • Experienced qualified lawyer
  • 10+ years PQE in Intellectual Property and Broadcasting Compliance
  • Familiarity with Intellectual Property Laws (essential)
  • Knowledge of broadcasting operations techniques
  • In-house experience with leadership skills and business acumen
  • Proven track record in broadcasting sector
  • Knowledge of Asian established and emerging markets (essential)
  • Command of an Asian language required
  • Ability to work unsupervised and cross-culturally

Benefits: Attractive financial package including weighted salary/bonus and significant career growth opportunities within the expanding CRC group.

Reference: LC/BRS/0401/17/001
Location: Portland House, Bressenden Place, London SW1E 5RS, Westminster
