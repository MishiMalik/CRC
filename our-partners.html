Our Strategic Partners

Building Excellence Through Collaboration

Discover the industry-leading organizations we partner with to deliver world-class compliance and risk management solutions

Evizone

CRC has partnered with Canadian-based communications and big-data management specialist Evizone, to provide comprehensive compliance archiving for regulated industries.

Founded in 2008, Evizone puts its customers back in control of their data with cutting edge offerings in Secure Communications, eDiscovery/eDisclosure, Compliance Archiving, and Governance. Their patented technology allows secure sharing without loss of controla major challenge for many organisations' risk management plans.

Evizone Communications Governance and Compliance Archiving is powerful, quick and easy for end users. It automates supervisory review, compliance, e-discovery, legal holds, case management, document redaction, and document destruction.

Compliant with: SEC, FINRA, Dodd-Frank, SOX, HIPAA, GDPR, FCA, PRA

Visit Evizone Website

Chartered Institute for Securities & Investment (CISI)

CRC has partnered with the Chartered Institute for Securities & Investment (CISI) to offer the highest standards of professional excellence in Compliance and Risk, Capital Markets, Corporate Finance, Financial Planning, Wealth Management, and Islamic Finance.

CISI, formed in 1992 by London Stock Exchange practitioners, has over 40,000 members across 116 countries. 40,000 exams are taken annually worldwide, with 15,000 outside the UK. CRC, through this partnership, enables practitioners in Africa to achieve core competence and strong careers in financial services and regulatory compliance.

Visit CISI Website View Certificate

Property Investors Consortium Ltd (PIC)

The Property Investors Consortium Ltd (PIC) is a UK-registered private limited company promoting and defending the interests of its members as property investors. CRC has provided expert advice on development projects in Dubai and helped ensure compliance with UAE Real Estate Regulations.

Visit PIC Website

Idox Compliance

Idox Compliance is a leading provider of compliance communication, training services, and consultancy. They integrate compliance into daily business, tailoring solutions for each organization to manage risk effectively. Their solutions are suitable for global players, SMEs, and public sector organizations.
