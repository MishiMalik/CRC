Who We Are and What We Do?

Compliance and Risk Consultancy (CRC) is the umbrella business organisation for five specialised subsidiary organisations: CRC Real Estate, Property Investors Consortium (PIC), Compliance Training Academy (CTA), Legal Complaints Services (LCS) and Consumer Protection Bureau (CPB).
